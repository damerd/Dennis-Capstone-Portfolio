Project Overview

ClaimIntel is an AI-powered application designed to assist insurance professionals by organizing claim information, identifying important facts, evaluating liability and exposure, and producing structured claim summaries. The project combines software engineering, artificial intelligence, databases, and decision-support techniques into a single platform focused on improving claim quality and consistency.

Current Features

Document Upload

Upload claim documents for automated review.

AI Analysis

Generate organized claim summaries using artificial intelligence.

PDF Reports

Produce professional claim review reports.

Coverage Review

Summarize policy information and claim details.

Liability Assessment

Evaluate comparative negligence and exposure.

Venue Analysis

Provide jurisdiction and venue considerations.
