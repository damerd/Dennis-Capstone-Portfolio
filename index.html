About This Portfolio

This ePortfolio represents my growth throughout the Computer Science program at Southern New Hampshire University. It highlights my development in software engineering, algorithms and data structures, databases, security, and professional communication.

The portfolio is centered around ClaimIntel, an AI-powered insurance claims analysis platform designed to support more consistent, organized, and data-informed claim reviews.

Featured Capstone Project: ClaimIntel

ClaimIntel is an AI-powered insurance claims analysis platform that reviews claim documents, identifies key facts, analyzes liability and exposure, and generates structured reports for claim professionals.

For my CS 499 capstone, I will enhance ClaimIntel in three areas: software engineering and design, algorithms and data structures, and databases.
